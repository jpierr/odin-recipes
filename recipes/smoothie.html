Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

This refreshing Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie is a delicious treat that's perfect for a quick breakfast or a midday snack.

Ingredients

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Chop Strawberries into blender
  2. Mix greek yogurt
  3. Blend
  4. Serve
